## 🚀 What is Essara?

**Essara** is an AI-powered personal finance command center designed specifically for the Indian market to provide total financial clarity. 

Its core purpose is to help users seamlessly track their spending, manage recurring subscriptions, and monitor their investment portfolios within a single, secure, and intuitive dashboard.

---

## ✨ Core Features

*   **🧾 AI Receipt Scanner:** Uses advanced OCR to instantly extract merchant details, amounts, and dates from photos of receipts with 98% accuracy.
*   **🔄 Subscription Manager:** Automatically identifies and tracks recurring charges to help you prevent unwanted renewals and save money.
*   **📈 Investment Tracking:** Real-time monitoring of stocks and mutual funds with rich visual performance charts.
*   **🧠 Smart AI Insights:** Provides actionable intelligence, such as anomaly detection and spending habit alerts.
*   **⚡ Native PWA Experience:** Offers a lightning-fast, app-like feel with offline capabilities and seamless cross-device syncing.

## 💡 The Value Proposition

Stop leaking money on forgotten subscriptions. Essara is designed to save users an average of **$340 annually** by identifying hidden and forgotten subscriptions.
